YOGA

Yoga, a science not less than 5000 years old has addressed a normal man to move towards higher states of harmony and peace both as an individual and also as a social being.

Change is not something that we should fear. Rather, it is something that we should welcome. For without change, nothing in this world would ever grow or blossom, and no one in this world would ever move forward to become the person they’re meant to be.

MEDITATION

Meditation is the process of systematically allowing the mind to become still for specific periods of time each day. Through meditation, the relationship between consciousness and the world gradually changes.

Agasyaa with Scientific research as it's base has used many of these techniques from the yoga lore and demonstrated it's value in promotion of positive health in several areas of society. Positive physical health in the form of improved muscle strength, stamina, dexterity, immune stability have been achieved after the integrated approach of yoga for positive physical health. Improved perceptual sharpness, intelligence, memory, emotional stability that are indicators of positive mental health have been achieved after yoga practices. Prevention of Many of the stress related health problems are possible through yoga practices. Large number of general public, professionals, executives etc, are drawn towards these Positive Physical health programmes not only to prevent the dreaded diseases like heart attacks and cancers, but also to promote their own family harmony and peace.

SWACHH BHARAT

Nearly 50 Agasya volunteers with the help of FAK-82 gathered on November 18th in Kavali to push PM Narendra Modi’s swachh Bharat initiative to another level altogether. Uptaking the task of maintaining cleanliness in India.

Agasya took its first step by cleaning Har Ki Podi. All Agasya volunteers made sure to contribute their bit in cleaning India. The idea behind this was to propagate the Swachh Bharat campaign, initiated by Prime Minister Narendra Modi, to reach out to maximum people and spread awareness amongst them.


CHILDREN EDUCATION

With an aim of working towards education, Agasya is funding the education of underprivileged children who otherwise cannot afford to go to private schools due to financial constraints. To help in children development Agasya.

is also providing milk and Biscuits everyday to the childrens who are coming to Yoga class regularly at Amaravathi Yogasramamu, Amaravathi, AP.

WATER ISSUES

Water and hygiene are the basic essentials of life. There are over 2billion people still using unsafe drinking water sources. With an attempt of controlling this, Agasya installed water coolers in schools, gurudwaras, mandirs and public.

markets to protect people from water borne diseases.

BLANKETS FOR THE NEEDY

Agasya thinks, its not about changing the lives of the people but doing something even more basic i.e making them survive, enabling them to fight their battle of survival against Mother Nature.

Agasya has a strong inner drive to do something for them and they started off by donating 500 blankets in slum areas of Kavali, Nellore Dist, AP. We are proud members of FAK-82, MKR Foundation and Amaravathi Yogasramamu, Being a part of such prestigious organizations will help us to do such a type of good social Activities in order to ensure a sustainable future for society.
